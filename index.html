Movie Bingo

Open your eyes to what movies actually say about the world

Movies portray the world as the movie creators see: with their implicit biases and discrimination. These biases are so casual you don't even notice when only the women seem to be always screaming at the first sign of danger, the non-white characters don't seem to last till the end of the movie, or how the protagonist is always a white male.

With Movie Bingo, you can watch out for these subtle assumptions and perpetuation of a predominantly white male view of the world and report on how this movie fares with this view. Each month we will watch a different movie together and mark all instances of such biases collectively. We can then view how the movie fared across a large cross-section of population and notice which demographic was cognizant of what bias.
